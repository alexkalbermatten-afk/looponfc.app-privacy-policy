1. Overview

This privacy policy describes how LoopO NFC ("the App"), developed by Alex Kalbermatten ("the Developer"), handles information. The App is designed with privacy as a core principle and operates entirely on your device without external data transmission.

2. Data Collection

The App does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is gathered
  • No usage analytics are collected
  • No device identifiers are accessed
  • No location data is tracked
  • No data is sent to external servers

All data created within the App (including NFC tag information) is stored exclusively on your iOS device and is never transmitted outside of it.

3. NFC Access

The App requires NFC (Near Field Communication) access to perform its core functionality of reading NFC tags. This access:

  • Is used only when the App is actively in use
  • Does not store NFC data beyond your immediate session
  • Does not transmit NFC data anywhere
  • Can be revoked at any time in your device Settings

4. No Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with or use:

  • Advertising networks (no ads)
  • Analytics services
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud services for data storage
  • Any external data processors

5. Contacting the Developer

If you choose to contact the Developer via email:

  • Your email and any information you provide will be used solely to respond to your inquiry
  • This information is not used for marketing purposes
  • Contact is optional and not required to use the App

6. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, its security depends on your iOS device's security features. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iOS device updated
  • Using a passcode or Face ID/Touch ID
  • Regularly backing up your device

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. The "Effective Date" at the top will indicate when changes were made. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
