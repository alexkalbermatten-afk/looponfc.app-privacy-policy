This privacy policy applies to the Android application "LoopO NFC" (hereafter "the App"), developed and published by Alex Kalbermatten (hereafter "the Developer").

The App does NOT collect or store any personal user data. All data entered or generated within the App is stored exclusively on your device's local storage. No data is transmitted to the Developer or any third parties.

3. Permissions and Device Access

The App requires the following permissions to function:

All permissions are used solely for the App's core functionality. No background data collection occurs.
